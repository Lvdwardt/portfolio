published: true
---

1. Go to [kiwi.com](https://www.kiwi.com/)
2. Choose a range of several days for the date if possible. This will increase your chances of finding a cheap flight.
3. Enter your departure point(s) and destination(s) or choose "anywhere" (If you live in the Netherlands, choose: AMS,EIN,MST,RTM,NRN).
4. Think about whether you need luggage and fill it in here.
5. Choose the best option for you. You can choose "direct flights only" in filters for the best options.
6. Once you have found a flight you can follow the following flowchart.
